Workshops


QuantEcon can organize and run remote and in-person workshops and short courses on quantitative economics and high-performance computing using Python and Julia. Past locations include the Econometric Society meetings, Columbia, Copenhagen, the Reserve Bank of Australia, Stanford, Princeton, Harvard, MIT, Berkeley, and UCLA.
